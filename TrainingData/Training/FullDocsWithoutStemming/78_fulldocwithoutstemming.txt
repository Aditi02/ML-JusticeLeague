Indian woman accused of selling baby for $ 130 .
NEW DELHI Indian CNN A woman has been charged for selling her newborn baby boy police in southern India said Tuesday .
The woman allegedly sold her child for rupees about $ 130 to an auto rickshaw driver because she wanted to marry someone else after the child father abandoned her according to police .
Prakash Jadhav of Khammam district police in Andhra Pradesh state told CNN that the child buyer had also been charged .
If convicted both the woman and the driver face up to 10 years in prison Jadhav said .
